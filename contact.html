FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by product. Tires: 100 sets / 1 x 20FCL. E-bikes: 20 units. EV parts: negotiable. Contact us for small sample orders.

How long does shipping take?

Sea freight to Africa (Zambia/Ethiopia): 25–35 days. Singapore: 8–14 days. Australia: 18–25 days. Air freight also available.

Do you offer OEM / custom branding?

Yes. We offer private label services for tires and e-bikes, including custom brand name, logo, packaging, and color options.

What payment terms do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C at sight, and Western Union. Typical terms: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

Can I get product samples first?

Yes, sample orders are available. Sample cost and freight are charged upfront and can be deducted from your first bulk order.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products hold ISO, CE, DOT, EU Label, RoHS, and EN15194 certifications depending on product type and destination market.
