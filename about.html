Our Story

Founded with a Mission to Bridge China and the World

CM IMPORT&EXPORT was established in 2012 with a straightforward mission: give international buyers the same access to Chinese factory pricing and quality that domestic companies enjoy — without the risk of working with unknown suppliers thousands of kilometres away.

We started with tires, building direct relationships with Triangle, Linglong, and Sailun factories in Shandong Province. Over the years we expanded into BYD new-energy vehicle parts and Sinotruk commercial truck components — always staying close to the factories and always putting quality inspection before cost-cutting.

Today, 127 active importers across Zambia, Singapore, Australia, Ethiopia, and Liberia trust us to handle their China sourcing end-to-end: from factory negotiation and SGS pre-shipment inspection through to export customs documentation and final delivery.

  • ✓ Direct partnerships with 50+ ISO 9001-certified Chinese factories
  • ✓ SGS-verified pre-shipment inspection on every order batch
  • ✓ China Customs registered exporter — faster clearance, fewer delays
  • ✓ Full trade terms covered: FOB, CIF, DAP, EXW
  • ✓ OEM & ODM customization — your brand, Chinese manufacturing cost
  • ✓ English-speaking team, WhatsApp response within 2 hours
